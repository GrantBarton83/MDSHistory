Spring Cleanup

It's Spring Cleaning Time again! Yes that's right, Saturday May 18th, we will be holding a clean-up party from 9:30 am - 1 pm. This is a great way to enjoy some fresh air, get your hands dirty, and put in those required work hours. We have some gardening tools, but please bring what you have a home for pruning, digging and raking. Hope to see you there!

It takes money to ensure that the dog park remains a safe and clean space for all who visit.
YOUR SUPPORT will go towards park improvements and on-going maintenance.

Melrose Dog Society

The Melrose Dog Society (MDS) is comprised of a small group of local citizens who are passionate about dogs and want to promote responsible dog ownership. Three years in the making, we are proud to announce the official grand opening of The Melrose Dog Park at Ell Pond on September 6, 2012
